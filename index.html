Owner Details

Late Shree Kailash Chand Agrawal(1955-2024). His personality says about all that hardwork, he has done in his life. His nature and behaviour promotes simple living and following brahmacharya routine. He was straight forward in conversation related to shop and was also environment making with friends. His origin village was Bagha, where he worked hard for more than 40 years running a shop. Then one of his son started his own business in the town near to the villge.Later he along with his family shifted to that town called Kagarol. But, his routine and working environment didn't change and contributed to his son's business by opening another shop in the same market and ran it successfully for 10 more years. In his life span, importance of work made a great positive impact on the life of his ancestors to be comfortable. Now, we are left with his memories. One of his ancestors now owns the shop named Vinod Provision Store which is now the oldest and reputed shop in the market and also nearby villages.
Mr. Vinod Kumar Agrawal, a hardworking son of Late Shree Kailash Chand Agrawal. He follows the discipline of his father. He learnt how to handle the shop from his father in the very early age of 18 since then he sharpened his mind to grow his business in the nearby town named kagarol by opening a small shop intitially, he worked hard continuously for 20 years and grown his business to popularity, earned a lot of name and fame. Now, he is one of the successful businessman in the town famous with the name Vinod Bagha Wale as the origin village was Bagha. He now alone handles the whole business giving his family a comfortable life.
